Stuck in Kitchen

Saturday, 27 November 2010 by Rob Li - permalink

Trey Ratcliff's work has been really inspiring me as of late.

There's nothing really special about the photos.  They were taken more so I could practice the tone mapping technique. I didn't go too crazy with the "HDR look" - but it was hard to hold back!

All I would note is that a tripod is a must!

I just love the ability to bring the texture of the wood out. (who said it was an ugly table? =P)
